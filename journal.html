02 · JOURNAL + DATA ANALYTICS

The trading journal that also stops you from breaking your rules.

Every trading journal does an excellent job of documenting the past: importing executions, tracking P&L, tagging setups, and helping you review what worked and what didn't. That analysis is invaluable — but by the time you're studying the trade, the decision has already been made.

MAKETZO adds what traditional journals are missing: a discipline layer that works alongside your journal in real time. It helps you stay aligned with your rules while the market is open, so your best trading decisions are supported not just in hindsight, but in the moments that matter most.
