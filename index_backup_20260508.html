The Command System

Four states. Zero guessing.

Maketzo Edge tells you exactly what to do on every candle. Your journal holds you accountable to it.

🟢
Prime Time
Your window is open. Cycle is turning. Momentum is building. Look for entries — this is where your best trades live.
🟠
Scale Out
Risk is increasing. Reduce position size. Lock in partial profits. Momentum may be turning against you.
🔴
Exit Now
The trade is broken. Close immediately. No hesitation. No averaging down. This is a hard rule — follow it.
⚫
No Trade
Conditions are not favorable. Sit on your hands. This is where most losses happen — learn to wait.
