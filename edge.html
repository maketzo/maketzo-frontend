03 · TRADE INTELLIGENCE

Know when your window is open. Know when to sit on your hands.

Most traders don't need more indicators. They need fewer bad trades. The real challenge is not finding another setup—it is knowing when momentum is truly on your side, when the odds are stacked against you, and when the smartest trade is no trade at all.

Maketzo Edge and Maketzo Pulse were built to provide that clarity. They highlight the narrow windows when your strategy has the greatest statistical tailwind and tell you when to sit on your hands. If your goal is to trade less, trade better, and let discipline do the heavy lifting, this is a practical place to start.

WHAT EDGE SOLVES

Three ways traders misread the day

Most losing sessions don't come from bad setups. They come from trading the wrong half of the day. Edge separates the windows that pay from the windows that punish.

PROBLEM 01
Trading the dead zones

Mid-day chop, post-news drift, slow afternoons. The hours when nothing real is happening are the hours most losses get manufactured. You're not making bad trades — you're trading when there's nothing to trade.

PROBLEM 02
Missing the prime windows

The first 30 minutes and the late-afternoon reversal. When conditions align, they don't stay aligned long. Traders who don't have a clear signal for "this is the window" hesitate through it.

PROBLEM 03
Holding past the exit

The setup worked, the move ran, you have profit on screen — and then you give it back. The window doesn't tell you when it closed. Edge does.
