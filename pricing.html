PRICING FAQ

Questions, answered straight

If something here is still unclear, email info@maketzo.co — we read every message.

Do I need a credit card to start the trial?

Yes. Card-on-file is how we keep the trial friction-free — no awkward "convert now" interruption on day 7. We don't charge anything until day 8, and you can cancel one-click from your account at any point before then.

What happens on day 8?

Your chosen plan's first charge runs — $19, $49, or $79 — and your trial converts to a full subscription. We email a reminder on day 6 so day 8 is never a surprise.

Can I cancel during the trial?

Yes, any time. One click from your account page ends the trial and prevents any charge. No phone call. No "let me transfer you." Done.

What's your refund policy?

If we charge you and you change your mind within 7 days of that charge, email info@maketzo.co and we'll refund — no friction. Beyond 7 days post-charge, cancellation stops future billing but past charges stand.

Can I switch between Journal, Pro, and Elite later?

Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated — you get the new features straight away and pay the difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle, so you keep what you paid for.

What about annual billing?

Annual saves you roughly 32% — Journal at $159/year, Pro at $399/year, Elite at $649/year. Same trial mechanics, same one-click cancel. Toggle "Annual" at the top of this page to lock in the price.

How do referral credits work?

Once you start your trial you get a personal link. Every trader who signs up through it and stays past day 8 earns you one month free on your next bill. Stack up to two free months per billing cycle. Manage all of this from your account.

Is my trading data private?

Yes. Your journal entries, rule-break data, P&L, and behavioral signals are yours alone. We never sell or share trading data with third parties. AI coaching features are opt-in only and can be disabled at any time. Full detail in our privacy policy.
