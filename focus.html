WHY STATE MATTERS

Your worst trades aren't analytical mistakes.

They're state failures. The trade looked fine. The chart was clean. The plan was right. But you were tired, angry, chasing — and the click happened anyway. State is the silent variable in every blow-up.

Threat 01

Overtrading

More clicks does not mean more edge. Boredom and screen-time pressure manufacture setups that don't exist.

Threat 02

Revenge Trading

The trade after the loss is rarely a trade. It's a settlement with the market. The market doesn't owe you anything.

Threat 03

Decision Fatigue

By hour three the brain stops weighing setups and starts pattern-matching. Quality drops. Conviction stays. That is the danger zone.

Threat 04

FOMO

The move you missed is not the move you would have caught. The replay in your head is louder than the chart in front of you.

Your greatest edge is not your setup. It is your ability to follow your setup.
