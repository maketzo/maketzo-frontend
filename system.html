STRIKE SYSTEM

Three strikes. Then the rule is the rule.

A single bad trade is human. A repeat pattern is a habit. The strike system lets you wobble — but only so far.

1
First Strike
COACH SAYS surfaces the rule you broke. Trading continues. You've been seen, not stopped.
2
Second Strike
Size reduces to 50%. The cost of breaking rules goes up before the day is lost. Most traders stop here.
3
Third Strike
24-hour lockout. New orders are blocked. You can journal, you can review — you can't trade.
Why three? Behavioral research is clear: a single warning gets ignored, a single stop feels punitive. Three steps gives you the room to be human while removing the room to spiral.
